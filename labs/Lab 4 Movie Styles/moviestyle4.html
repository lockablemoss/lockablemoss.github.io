Synopsis

Remy dreams of becoming a great chef, despite being a rat in a definitely rodent-phobic profession. He moves to Paris to follow his dream, and with the help of hapless garbage boy Linguini he puts his culinary skills to the test in the kitchen but he has to stay in hiding at the same time, with hilarious consequences. Remy eventually gets the chance to prove his culinary abilities to a great food critic but is the food good? A Pixar animation.

Review

Ratatouille is one of the greatest animated films of all time. This movie is packed so full of charm that it makes me want to watch it over and over again. Who knew a movie about a cooking rat would be so amazing. They make you feel bad for a rat. That I find crazy. The movie looks amazing and watching them cook in this movie will make you hungry. They make the relationship between Remy and Linguini perfect. I dont really think this movie has any flaws and that it is perfect.
