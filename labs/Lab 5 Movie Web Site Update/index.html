Synopsis

An anthology of six short films that take place in 19th-century post-Civil War era during the settling of the Old West.

Directors

This film was written and directed by Ethan and Joel Coen and released in the US on November 16, 2018.

Review

The Ballad of Buster Sruggs is an antholegy of 6 shorts that take place in the west post civil war. This movie is a fantastic example of how anthologies should be made. With each story being funny, sad, and tense, this movie captures the western feel perfectly. Because not everything is just cowboys, they throw in things like prospectors and entertainers. The acting in the movie is fantastic and makes the movie very immersive. The different settings also show the many sides of the west that most movies don't show. I would rate this movie 4/5 and would recomend anyone who likes westerns to check it out.
