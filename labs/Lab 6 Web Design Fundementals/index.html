The Principles of Deisgn are ways to use elements together

  • Movement
  • Balance
  • Emphasis
  • Unity

Movement

Movement is the use of lines, color, and repetition to create the illusion of motion.

This image shows the use of Movement with the smoke trails behind the planes. The the angle of the planes and the curve of the smoke also shows movement.

Balance

Balance is the act of comparing or estimating two things, one against the other, and the contrast between them.

This image shows the use of Balance by showing repitition with the fabric weaving in and out of itself.

Unity

Unity is the correct balance of composition of color that produces a harmonious effect.

This image shows Unity by showing the flowers and the scissors as the main focus of the image, meaning they are meant to go together.

Emphasis

Emphasis is to express with a particular stress or force.

This image shows Emphasis by having the bright green car the the lighter green background, bringing your attention immediatly to the car before anything else.
